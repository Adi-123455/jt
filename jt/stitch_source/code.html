The Acting Journey

Look, I'm just a simple guy from Queens with a dream and a very high metabolism. Since '94, I've been hitting the pavement, memorizing lines, and making sure my headshot looks exactly like me—only slightly more mysterious.

Acting isn't just a job; it's a feeling. Like when you see a meatball sub from across the street and you just *know* it's the one.

I've played everything from a dying man in a box to the greatest neurosurgeon Salem has ever seen. If you need someone who can cry on cue (especially if the script mentions a dropped pizza), I'm your guy.
