About Joey

The actor with Queens confidence and magazine-spread charisma.

Joey built his career on instinct, hustle, and a very strong belief that every room is better when he walks into it. His style blends old-school TV-star charm with a playful warmth that makes even the biggest performance feel personal.

Whether he is delivering a soap-opera monologue, making a room laugh, or turning a simple line into a catchphrase, Joey has a knack for making moments stick.

"I can play anything. Doctor, detective, action hero... even a guy who pretends he knows what he's doing."
